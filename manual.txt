S-PIVOT V0.000

BUG IN FIREFOX

If the skeleton simulation starts behaving strangely, it is likely
due to a bug in Firefox 19.

Solution: Use Chrome.

OVERVIEW

Left mouse button
	Move pivot with skeleton simulation

Left mouse button + Shift
	Move pivot without skeleton simulation

Q
	Add line
	Confirm with Enter
	Cancel with Backspace

E
	Add circle
	Confirm with Enter
	Cancel with Backspace

S
	Toggle rigid pivot
	Prevents connected pivots from rotating freely

D
	Toggle lock pivot
	Locks the pivot to current coordinate

X
	Delete pivot with connected shapes

LINK LIMITS

Most modern browsers support maximum 2000 characters in the link.
